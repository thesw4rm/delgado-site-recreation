How does eLearn work?

eLearn is a free online program to help you achieve your goals in school, work, and life.

Orientation

Start with Orientation to learn about the program and take a pre-test to see how you are doing in English and Math and what to study online.

Classes

New classes in reading, writing, social studies, science, and math, start every month. Take what interests you and will help you reach your goals.

Testing

It’s always up to you to decide when you’re ready for the real test. Try a free practice test at home, and talk with your coach to register for the real exam. And if you don’t pass, that’s okay! You can keep studying and try again.

Graduation

After you pass the test, we are here to help you get ready for college, apply for jobs, and move on in your educational journey.
